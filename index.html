About Abhinav

Developer • Innovator • Problem Solver

Abhinav Ranjan (also known as itzmeabhinavranjan) is a young technology enthusiast from Muzaffarpur, Bihar, India. Known for his early interest in cybersecurity and ethical hacking, he began exploring digital security, networks, and ethical hacking concepts at a very young age.

Abhinav is recognized for teaching cybersecurity and ethical hacking concepts to students and beginners, making complex digital security topics easier to understand. His work focuses on promoting safe digital practices, vulnerability awareness, and responsible hacking education.

For his early achievements and contributions in cybersecurity education, he has been recognized as one of the youngest ethical hacking teachers and has received recognition from the Golden Book of World Records and Guinness World Records.

Along with cybersecurity, his work also spans across software development, technology systems, and digital innovation. As the founder of the Luminary Technicals ecosystem, he focuses on building projects related to secure infrastructure, web technologies, and modern digital solutions.

His vision is to help build a safer and more aware digital world. Driven by curiosity, discipline, and ethical responsibility, he continues to learn, build, and share knowledge with the next generation of technology enthusiasts. 🚀
